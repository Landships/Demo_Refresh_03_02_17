"How to use Sample Tank"

shopmaster@chobi-glass.com

You can find finished tank prefabs in the "Resources" >> "Sample_Tank_##" folder.


(1) Drag and drop the prefab into the Hierarchy window.
   
(2) Select the top object of the tank in the Hierarchy window.

(3) Choose the "Tank ID" differing from other tanks in the Inspector window.
   
    (Notice.) You can skip this step.

(4) Choose the "Relationship".
    Choose the relationship with other tanks.
   

(5) Select the top object of the tank, and adjust the position as you will.
   

(6) Now you are ready to play.
    (Notice.) For using AI tanks, please also read the how-to text "How to use AI Tank".
